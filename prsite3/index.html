Farzana Anowar

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science, University of Regina, and Mitacs Accelerate intern at Ericsson Canada. Previously, I was dealing with imbalanced bidding data to classify the fraud bidders in e-auction at e-Bay. Now, my research focuses on virtual and cost-sensitive incremental and decremental learning to classify the fraud data for e-commerce sites in real-time. Thus, my research is mainly focused on Data Mining, Machine Learning, Neural Network, Knowledge Discovery, and Artificial Intelligence. Also, I'm working in a project partnering with one of the leading telecommunications companies to handle high dimensional network data.
